Producer Composer Choreographer Songwriter Music teacher Music therapist Conductor

What hours do musicians work?

Musicians often work irregular hours. If they're touring, they could spend most of their day traveling, then spend time setting up at the venue, completing a sound check then performing.

What's the difference between a singer and a musician?

Many people use the terms singer and musician interchangeably. While there's no formal difference between the two, some people make certain distinctions. For some, singers only perform songs and musicians both write and perform. Musicians play instruments while singers only sing.

What is the work environment of a musician?

Musicians don't have a set work environment. They record music in a studio, practice in a designated space and perform on a stage. Additionally, they can write music anywhere. Depending on the instrument they play, they could spend most of their time standing, though piano players and drummers need to sit.
